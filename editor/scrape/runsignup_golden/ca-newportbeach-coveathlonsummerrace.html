Description

THURSDAY NIGHT SUMMER RACING AT NEWPORT DUNES! 

Don't miss the action-packed, community-spirited races. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned athlete, Cove offers something for everyone! Join us for fun, fitness and great prizes during the series!

 

THURSDAY NIGHT RACE SCHEDULE:  

Cove 1 - July 11th

Cove 2 - July 18

Cove 3 - August 1

Cove 4 - August 8 + Awards Party after the Race!

 

RACE DAY CHECK-IN: 4:30 - 6PM

RACE STARTS AT 6:15PM

COVE #1 - FREE RACE PHOTOS:  https://www.wildglassphotobuyphotos.com/COVEATHLON-1-2024

COVE #2 - FREE RACE PHOTOS: https://www.wildglassphotobuyphotos.com/COVEATHLON-2-2024

Choose from a variety of race options: 

  • 1K Swim + 5K Run Race
  • 1K Swim + 5K Run Relay 
  • 5K Run Race  
  • 1K Swim Race

EACH RACER RECEIVES:

  • Stylish Race T-shirt
  • Silicone Swim Cap (excludes 5K Runners unless you want to run in a swim cap!)
  • Free Race Photos (digital downloads to share on social media)
  • Chip Timing
  • Series Awards for Top 3 Age Groupers 
  • Series Awards for Top Clubs
  • Special long-sleeve t-shirt for athletes that complete all 4 races

Don't wait to register! We cap each race at 250 athletes - we will sell out!

Come experience the coolest summer race series & be part of an inspiring community!
