Description

Ready to flex your muscles at the wildest workout spot in town?  The Louisville Zoo and Flex Appeal invite you to join us for one of our exciting fitness classes at the Zoo.  All fitness levels are welcome to take part in this unique event. Each workout will be approximately one hour in length and will take place in different areas of the Zoo.


Sign up now to experience one of our wild classes!

Because of the unique atmosphere and the terrain around the Zoo, closed toed shoes are strongly encouraged.  Be sure to bring a water bottle-with dates in July, August and September we'll have you sweating before the workouts even start!  The Zoo has several bottle refill stations around the park so you should have a fill spot nearby! 

YOGA

Strike a pose inspired by some of our animal inhabitants and escape from everyday life during our unique outdoor yoga class. From beginners to seasoned practitioners, our yoga classes are designed to help you increase flexibility, strengthen muscles, and improve your overall health and recovery. 

 

Monday, July 1 | Giraffe

Find inspiration in nature as you stretch and strike a pose with our giraffe herd looking on. These gentle giants will soothe your mind and help you find inner peace after a long day.

 

Friday, August 2 | Glacier Run

You’ll BEARly believe how tranquil the award-winning arctic exhibit space Glacier Run can be after the Zoo closes. You may even have a chance to see one of our resident bears strolling through town.

 

Tuesday, September 3 | Snow Leopard

Take in the beauty of our serene, Himalayan-themed exhibit space, Snow Leopard Pass. You may catch a glimpse of one of the most elusive and graceful members of the animal kingdom.

Wild Workout FAQ's


Where will Wild Workouts be held?  The Louisville Zoo at 1100 Trevilian Way, Louisville, KY 40213.


Where will I park?  Parking for Wild Workouts will be in the main parking lot in front of the Zoo.


Where on Zoo grounds will the Wild Workouts be held?  Each Wild Workout class will be held in different areas throughout the Zoo that are suitable for the type of workout being completed.  All classes will meet on the Front Plaza of the Zoo, just inside the entrance gates. 


Will I have to pay for parking?  No, parking is included with your class reservation fee.


What time do the Wild Workouts start?  Your class will begin promptly at 6:30 pm.


What time should I arrive?  Please arrive between 6:00 and 6:15 pm on the date of your reservation. At 6:15 pm your class warm-up will begin. At 6:30 pm the Zoo gates will close and no other admittance will be allowed.


What if I will be late for my scheduled class?  If you are late to your scheduled class you will not be permitted entrance. The Zoo will be closed for this special after-hours event and staff focus will be on the class. Please arrive between 6:00 and 6:15 pm.


If I miss a class will I have a chance to make it up?  There will be no make-up dates for Wild Workouts.


Can I get a refund of my registration fee?  Sorry, registration fees are for the benefit of the charity and are non-refundable.


Will there be childcare available?  There will not be childcare available during these after-hours events.


Can my child attend Wild Workouts with me?  Yes, your child may participate in Wild Workouts with you. However, your child will need to have their own registration. The parent or guardian will need to sign a waiver on the child’s behalf during their registration.


Do I need to be a member of the Louisville Zoo or Flex Appeal to register for Wild Workouts?  No, you do not have to be a member of the Louisville Zoo or Flex Appeal to register for Wild Workouts. Everyone is welcome! 


Can I register for multiple/all of the classes offered?  Yes, you can, but you will have to register for each class separately.

 

What happens if it rains?  Wild Workouts are rain or shine events. In the event of rain or other inclement weather your instructor may move your class into one of the Zoo's indoor spaces.


How old should participants be?  Participants should be 12 and older.


Can my child attend a Wild Workout alone?  For the safety of your child we require a parent or guardian register with anyone under 18.


Is there a discount for children?  No discounts are offered at this time.
