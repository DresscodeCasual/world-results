Description

5k for the Kids: A virtual race that can be run anywhere benefiting Boston Children's Hospital.

Race Details:
Upon registering you will be mailed a 5k training plan, (5k run/walk, 5k novice, and a 5k advanced plan) along with a race bib. This is a virtual race. Run or walk 3.1 miles any time, and any place you want during the month of July. All ages and abilities welcome. Complete the distance, and submit your results. All participants will receive a finisher's medal.

Run for the Kids:
This race benefits Boston Children's Hospital, and every dollar raised funds breakthrough research, family support services, and vital care not covered by insurance through the hospital’s Every Child Fund. Funds will give children battling the most complex diseases treatments they can’t find anywhere else; help children across the world get a second chance; fuel 3,000+ researchers working to find cures; help patient-families in crisis with urgent needs, including housing, food and clothing; bring crucial care to families through outpatient psychiatry clinics; and so much more.

Additional Donations:
If you would like to make an additional donation outside of your entry fee to Boston Children's Hospital, please use the donation tab at the top right of this page.

Race Results:
To post your finish time click on the Results Tab, and click on Submit Virtual Results. 

Thank you for supporting Boston Children's Hospital and enjoy your race!
